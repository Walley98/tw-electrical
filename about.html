About us

Built on craftsmanship & compliance.

We're a small, trusted electrical contractor serving homeowners, businesses and industrial clients. Every installation is carried out by qualified engineers — and finished to a standard we'd be proud to put our own name on.

Our story

Years on the tools, thousands of installations.

TW Electrical Contractor was founded on a simple principle: do the job right the first time. Over more than 15 years we've delivered everything from single socket replacements to full commercial fit-outs and industrial 3-phase distribution boards.

We stay small on purpose. It means every job gets hands-on attention, every client gets a direct line to the engineer doing the work, and every installation is signed off personally.
