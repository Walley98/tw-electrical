Homes, rewires & upgrades

Whether you're renovating, extending or just updating tired wiring, we handle the full scope of residential work. Clean, code-compliant, and finished with care.

Offices, retail & hospitality

Minimise downtime and maximise safety. We deliver commercial fit-outs, maintenance contracts and compliance testing with a schedule you can plan around.

  • Fault finding & remedial work
  • Planned maintenance contracts
